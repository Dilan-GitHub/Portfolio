Sales Consultant

R&D Auto Sales LLC

From April 2014 to July 2016, I worked as a Sales Consultant at R&D Auto Sales LLC in Garland, TX. I guided customers through the car-buying process, understanding their needs and providing personalized recommendations based on their budget and lifestyle. I managed sales negotiations, securing the best prices, trade-ins, and financing options while meeting sales targets. I maintained expert knowledge of vehicle models and industry trends, ensuring a seamless purchasing experience from inquiry to final paperwork. My commitment to strong customer relationships and post-sale support boosted retention and referrals, honing my skills in negotiation, customer engagement, and product expertise.

Finance Officer

R&D Auto Sales LLC

As a Finance Officer, I guided customers through the vehicle purchasing process by managing their financing needs. I evaluated customer creditworthiness to recommend suitable financing options and provided clear explanations of loan terms and payment plans to ensure informed decisions. My role involved accurately processing finance applications, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, and collaborating with the sales team to align financing solutions with purchases. I also promoted finance products like extended warranties and insurance while maintaining strict adherence to state and federal regulations. This experience strengthened my skills in financial analysis, customer service, and regulatory compliance, delivering a seamless financing experience for customers.

R&D Auto Sales LLC
